### FULL POST

Most engineering teams hate writing LinkedIn posts.

Not because they can't write. Because the feedback loop is broken.

You write a draft. Send it on Slack. Someone says "looks good." It gets posted. Gets 12 likes. Nobody knows why.

We changed one thing: we preview posts in a tool that looks like the actual LinkedIn feed.

Suddenly everyone has opinions. "The hook is too long." "That image doesn't work on mobile." "The second paragraph says the same thing as the first."

Better feedback = better posts. Better posts = more reach. More reach = more pipeline.

It's not about the tool. It's about making the invisible visible.
